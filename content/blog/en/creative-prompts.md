# Mastering Creative Prompts in PhotoMaker

The key to exceptional AI-generated photos lies in crafting the perfect prompt. Let's explore how to write prompts that produce amazing results.

## Understanding Prompt Structure

A good prompt typically includes:

1. Style description
2. Lighting details
3. Mood or atmosphere
4. Technical specifications

## Example Prompts

Here are some effective prompt examples:

```
Professional corporate headshot, natural window lighting, confident pose, neutral background, sharp focus, 35mm lens
```

```
Cinematic portrait, golden hour lighting, dramatic shadows, shallow depth of field, anamorphic lens flare
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Being too vague
- Contradictory descriptions
- Overcomplicating the prompt
- Forgetting technical details

## Advanced Techniques

Learn to combine different elements:

- Mix photography styles
- Experiment with lighting conditions
- Add specific camera settings
- Include post-processing effects

Remember, practice makes perfect. Keep experimenting with different combinations to find what works best for your style.
